# Golden Dragon AI Studio Website Map

This document describes the public website structure and navigation relationships.
It is a logical map for QA, internal linking planning, and future AI assistant routing.
It is not `sitemap.xml` and does not implement SEO metadata.

## Public Pages

- `/` — Home
- `/chat` — Dragon Chat
- `/services` — Services
- `/services/portfolio` — Projects / Portfolio
- `/solutions` — AI Solutions
- `/solutions/health-support-ai` — Health Support AI
- `/solutions/animal-health` — Equine Health AI
- `/solutions/underwater-ai` — Underwater Inspection AI
- `/solutions/quantum-trading-ai` — Quantum Trading AI
- `/solutions/luxury-concierge-ai` — Luxury Concierge AI
- `/news` — News

## Global Navigation

- Home links to `/`
- AI Solutions links to `/solutions`
- AI Solutions dropdown links to:
  - `/solutions/health-support-ai`
  - `/solutions/animal-health`
  - `/solutions/underwater-ai`
  - `/solutions/quantum-trading-ai`
  - `/solutions/luxury-concierge-ai`
- Services links to `/services`
- Services dropdown links to:
  - `/services/portfolio`
- Language selector switches the current language across the same route
- Dragon Chat links to `/chat`
- News links to `/news`
